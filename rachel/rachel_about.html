Thank you for visiting my portfolio!

So.. Why design? As seen in my focus of study, I initially entered USC with the intent of pursuing law. While I loved the relationships I was able to form within the program and the study of law, I felt the nature of law too restrictive and inflexible.

I decided I needed to be at a place where creativity and building solutions are prioritized - and after a long academic journey, I finally discovered the field of User Experience. I truly love what I do - I get to maximize my creative outlets while also building skills in a field ----
